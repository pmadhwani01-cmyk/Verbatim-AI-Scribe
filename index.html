Privacy Policy

Graindrop records audio locally on your device. Recordings are processed using your own API keys and are never stored on our servers. We do not collect, share, or sell your data.

Delete Your Data

All recordings and transcripts are stored locally on your device. To delete all data, uninstall the app.
