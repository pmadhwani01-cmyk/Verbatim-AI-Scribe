Privacy Policy

Verbatim AI Scribe  ·  Effective date: May 3, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

The short version: Your recordings, transcripts, and meeting summaries never leave your device. Verbatim AI Scribe processes AI using your own API keys. The only data we or our partners collect relates to your subscription and app stability — never your meeting content.

Overview

Verbatim AI Scribe is an AI-powered meeting recorder, transcriber, and summariser for iOS. All audio recordings, transcripts, and AI-generated summaries are stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers that store your meeting content.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data never leaves your iPhone unless you explicitly export or share it:

Audio files (.m4a) Transcripts AI summaries Meeting metadata Chat history

This data is stored in a local SQLite database in your app's private document directory, protected by iOS Data Protection. Your API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain via AES-256 encryption and never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Groq (transcription)

When you transcribe a recording, audio is sent to Groq's API using your own Groq API key. Groq does not retain audio beyond processing. Verbatim AI Scribe does not receive or store this data.

Anthropic (AI summarisation)

When you generate a summary or use AI chat, transcript text is sent to Anthropic's API using your own API key. Verbatim AI Scribe does not receive or store this data.

AssemblyAI (speaker diarisation — optional)

If you enable speaker diarisation, audio is sent to AssemblyAI using your own API key. This feature is off by default and entirely optional.

RevenueCat (subscriptions)

Verbatim AI Scribe uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat collects an anonymous user ID, device identifiers, and purchase history for entitlement verification.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because your meeting data lives on your device, you are in complete control. Delete individual recordings from the Library screen. Delete all data by uninstalling the app. Subscription data held by RevenueCat is subject to their retention policy.

Children's Privacy

Verbatim AI Scribe is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
